# 🌼 Lazy Daisy

**Lazy Daisy** is a Flutter-based productivity and lifestyle app designed to help you build better habits, track your mood, manage expenses, and stay on top of the moments that matter — all in one place.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🧠 Habit Tracking
- Create and manage daily habits with customizable categories (Health, Work, Finance, Personal, and more)
- Set individual difficulty levels — Easy, Medium, or Hard — and earn XP points for every completion
- Level up through a gamified XP system that rewards consistency
- Visual streak tracking with a heatmap showing your progress over time

### 🔔 Smart Reminders
- Set a unique daily reminder time for each habit individually
- Notifications fire at your chosen time every day to keep you on track

### 📋 Habit Templates
- Get started instantly with 35+ pre-built habits across 7 curated packs including Morning Routine, Fitness, Study, and more

### 🏆 Challenges
- Take on weekly and monthly challenges to push your limits and maintain momentum

### 📊 Progress Analytics
- View detailed analytics including weekly trends, monthly completion rates, and streak data — all based on your real habit history

### 😊 Mood Tracker
- Log your daily mood and gain insights into patterns over time
- Weekly mood graph reveals the connection between your habits and how you feel

### 📅 Calendar
- A full calendar view of your habits and events with clean visual indicators for completed days

### 📓 Notepad
- A built-in notepad with a full-screen editor to jot down thoughts, goals, and reflections

### 💰 Expense Tracker
- Choose between a Student or Professional profile for tailored expense categories
- Track income and expenses with a running balance, monthly summary, and spending breakdown by category
- Supports multiple currencies: ৳ BDT, $ USD, € EUR, ₹ INR, ₩ KRW

### 🎂 Birthday Calendar
- Never miss a birthday — add friends and family with custom emoji avatars
- A calendar view with birthday dots and upcoming birthday banners on the home screen
- Optional day-before reminder notifications

### 🤖 AI Habit Coach *(Premium — Coming Soon)*
- Personalized AI-powered habit suggestions based on your goals and patterns

### 💾 Backup & Restore
- Keep your data safe with built-in backup and restore functionality

### 📱 Social Sharing
- Share your streak milestones as beautifully designed cards to Instagram, WhatsApp, and more

## 🛠 Built With

- **Flutter** — cross-platform mobile framework
- **Firebase** — authentication and cloud sync
- **Isar** — fast local database
- **Provider** — state management
- **flutter_local_notifications** — habit reminders
- **Google Fonts** — Raleway typography

## 🚀 Getting Started

```bash
git clone https://github.com/meeddhaa/lazy_daisy.git
cd lazy_daisy
flutter pub get
flutter pub run build_runner build --delete-conflicting-outputs
flutter run
```

> Firebase configuration files (`google-services.json`, `GoogleService-Info.plist`, `firebase_options.dart`) are not included in this repository. You will need to connect your own Firebase project using `flutterfire configure`.
